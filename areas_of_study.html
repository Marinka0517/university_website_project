Areas of Study

Choose your magical path at Southern Hogwarts University.

👻

Ghoul Studies

Learn to interact with spirits, ghosts, and other spectral beings. Protective wards, ghost etiquette, and ethical conjuring are all part of the curriculum. A favorite among those who like late-night library visits.

Requirements:

  • Affinity for water and emotions
  • Elemental Foundations completed
  • Basic Understanding of marine life

💧

Water Magic

Manipulate water in all its forms, streams, rain, or a flood. Emphasising healing Waters and aquatic creatures.

Requirements:

  • Affinity for water and emotions
  • Elemental Foundations completed
  • Basic Understanding of marine life

⚡

Thunder and Lightning

Command the raw furry of storms. Students will learn to summon lightning, understand storm patterns, and practice energy controll.

Requirements:

  • Highly magical sensitivity
  • Advanced Energy Theory completed
  • Quick Reflexes and stamina

🔮

Arcane Studies

Dive deep into the forces of magic. Learn to create your own spells, study ancient tomes, and get lost in the library. Challenging but prestigious.

Requirements:

  • Exceptional magical aptitude
  • All core subjects completed with exilence
  • Proficiency in multiple magical disiplines
  • Approval from Dean of Arcane Studies
