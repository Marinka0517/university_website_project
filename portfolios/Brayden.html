Dean of Ghoul Studies

Dr. Brayden Peterson

Dr. Brayden Peterson has been the Dean of Ghoul Studies since 2019, earning his PhD in Necromancy from the famous Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry. With his vast knowledge of necromancy, Dr. Peterson has worked tirelessly since to advance magical techniques in order to innovate the magical world and separate us further from the extreme ordinary of the muggles.

Dr. Peterson's main focus throughout his courses is to uncover the potential of ghouls and how they can help wizards and witches across the world accomplish great feats. He believes that necromancy and the studies of all kinds of ghouls may be the key to uncovering the roots of magic.

Lectures are open to students and outside parties who are pure-bloods only.

GS-1010: Introduction to Ghoul Studies
An introduction course to the studies of ghouls and necromancy covering the biology, behavior, and role of ghouls in the magical ecosystem.
GS-1020: Ghoul History
This course deeply explores the history and background of how the will of ghouls came to be harnessed and the power of the undead has been progressively improving with the advances in magical techniques.
GS-2010: Intoduction to Necromancy
An introduction course to commanding the dead. Necromancy is about transfering the flow of energy to the will of the dead. In this course we cover the very basics, starting with simple clay golems.
