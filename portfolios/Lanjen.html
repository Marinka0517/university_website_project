About Dr. Hsieh

I am the Dean of Water at Southern Hogwarts University.

Water is a basic element of life, and I study how its flow and energy can be used in magic.

I encourage my students to look beneath the surface and learn the quiet power that water carries.

Teaching Philosophy

Water holds many forms of power, and much of it is still unknown.

Through learning and exploration, we can open new doors and understand the world in different ways.

Water can heal, defend, or fight. It depends on how it is shaped.

That is why water magic is a wide and meaningful skill, and I guide my students to discover it at their own pace.

Notable Achievements

  • Developer of a basic “Water Flow Control” training method for beginner mages.
  • Serves as a water-based emotional guide, helping students connect their feelings with magic in a healthy way.
  • Wrote a short series of articles about how to live more mindfully with water in everyday life.
  • Designed the campus water cycle plan for Southern Hogwarts University to reduce water waste and encourage reuse.

Courses Taught
