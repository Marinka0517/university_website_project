Course Information

In this class, students are divided by two classes: The Battle class and The Life exploration class.

・Battle class

In this class, students primarily learn offensive magic. For example, they can train to cloak their bodies in electricity or unleash bursts of lightning around them. This class is suited for those who enjoy physical activity, as it involves a great deal of practical, hands-on training.

・Life exploration class

This class focuses mainly on light magic. Since light can illuminate the surroundings as well as generate heat, students learn how to apply these abilities in everyday life. The class emphasizes practical uses of magic that support daily living.
