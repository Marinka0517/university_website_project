Dr. Riley McCurdy

Dean of Arcane Magic - Southern Hogwarts University

Arcane Scholar & Mentor

Dr. Riley McCurdy has served as Dean of Arcane Magic for over twelve years. A prodigy in metaphysical spell construction, Dr. McCurdy earned their doctorate in Advanced Divination from the Obsidian Athenaeum at the age of twenty four, the youngest in the institution's history.

Their research bridges classical incantation theory with modern chaos-magic frameworks, yilding breakthoughs in sustainable spell-weaving and ethical enchantment design. Under their leadership, the Arcane Studies department has become a becon for innovators and traditionalists alike.

Teaching Philosopy

"Magic is not merely power it is responsibility, precision, and poetry. I challenge my students not to cast spells, but to understand why they work, how they shape reality, and what they reveal about the caster's soul."

Notable Achivements

  • Author of The Weave and the Word: Foundations of Ethical Spellcraft (2023)
  • Recipient of the International Order of Silver Quill (2021)
  • Developed the "Luminous Syntax" curriculum adopted by 14 magical universities worldwide
  • Pioneer in non-verbal, intent-based arcana
