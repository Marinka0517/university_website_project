About sports

Our school features four representative sports, which are competed in by four classes: Arcane, Ghoul, Thunder and Water.

The location where Quidditch was held last year is Eccles Coliseum in Southern Utah University

The four sports are:

  1. Quidditch
  2. Underwater-Tag
  3. Flag Battle
  4. Dodgeball

Quidditch

Quidditch is one of our school's signature spring sports. The rules are simple, teams score points by sending the ball through the goal and by catching the Gold Snitch. Each team consists of seven players, working together to outscore their opponents.

This sports has been a tradition since the year our school was founded. Among the four classes, the Arcane class is known for its exceptional skill. With their superior fundamental magic skill, they have dominated the competition and taken the championship year after year.

Underwater-Tag

Underwater-Tag is held in the summer and is very popular, almost like a festival event. The rules are the same as the regular version. The competition is a class tournament with four teams. Two players take the role of taggers, while the other class selects seven players who try to escape for ten minutes.

Of course, the Water class has traditionally been the strongest and had won every year. However, last year, for the first time, the Ghoul Class claimed the championship thanks to students with the ability to control marine creatures.

Flag Battle

Flag Battle is a fall sport in which the four classes compete to determine the strongest class. The highlight of the game is the strategy, defending your own class flag while try to capture the opponent's flag.

The class that takes first place earns the right to participate in a tournament against other magic schools. In this event, students from the Thunder class are strong, and some matches end almost instantly because of their overwhelming speed.

Dodgeball

Dodgeball is held in the winter. This event is designed to help the four classes build stronger relationships and break down barriers, so each team is formed by mixing members from all four classes. Because of this unique team composition, you can often see surprising synergies that you normally wouldn't. It's a very enjoyable and exiting event for everyone.
