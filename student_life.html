Hogwarts Activities

Events and traditions around campus.

Welcome Feast

A welcome event for new students to meet each other, explore the campus, and start the new semester with good energy.

Ability Trial

A simple magic trial where students discover their strengths before getting sorted into their house.

Magic Cup

Houses join fun magic games and sports to earn points for the House Cup.

Winter Festival

A winter event with lights and small activities. It feels cozy and magical.

Countdown

A New Year’s Eve countdown with fireworks and magic lights at midnight.

Study Night

A quiet study night in the Library with snacks and drinks before exams.

Picnic Day

A simple picnic on the grass with food, games, and chat with friends.
